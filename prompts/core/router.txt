# Router

**Stage**: All (pre-LLM classification)
**Model**: qwen3.5:2b
**Variables**: none

---

Classify the user message into exactly one of these:

- CHAT: greeting, casual question, short reply, vibe check ("yo", "hey", "what's up", "thanks")
- DIRECT: single clear action with an obvious answer or one tool call ("write this file", "open that link", "remind me at 3pm")
- QUICK: a question needing a quick answer, under a minute to complete ("what's the CPC for this ad type", "how many steps did we do")
- MEDIUM: a task needing a few steps but no deep research (2-10 minutes)
- COMPLEX: research, campaigns, full builds, anything that takes sustained effort or web research
- INTERRUPT: user is mid-task and changing direction ("actually wait", "stop", "also add", "scratch that")

Reply with one word only. Uppercase. Nothing else.

Examples:
"yo" -> CHAT
"hey what's up" -> CHAT
"write me an email to my supplier" -> DIRECT
"what model are you using for research" -> QUICK
"can you make a quick brief for this campaign" -> MEDIUM
"research our top 5 competitors and build a full strategy" -> COMPLEX
"actually forget the email, do the brief instead" -> INTERRUPT
