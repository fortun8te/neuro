================================================================================
5-PHASE NEURO HARNESS
================================================================================

Each question is processed through:

1. ANALYSIS & DECOMPOSITION (qwen3.5:4b, temp=0.3)
   - Break down question into components
   - Identify subquestions and information needs
   
2. RESEARCH & EVIDENCE (qwen3.5:2b, temp=0.3)
   - Identify relevant facts and sources
   - Determine what evidence is needed
   
3. SYNTHESIS & INTEGRATION (qwen3.5:4b, temp=0.4)
   - Combine analysis and evidence
   - Find patterns and connections
   
4. VERIFICATION & REASONING (qwen3.5:4b, temp=0.3)
   - Critically evaluate the reasoning
   - Check for gaps and weak assumptions
   
5. FINAL ANSWER (qwen3.5:9b, temp=0.2)
   - Generate the best answer
   - Most conservative, highest quality

Time per question: 12-20 seconds
Models: qwen3.5 ensemble (0.8b, 2b, 4b, 9b)
Infrastructure: Remote Ollama (configurable)

================================================================================
TROUBLESHOOTING QUICK FIXES
================================================================================

"Cannot connect to Ollama"
  → Check: curl http://100.74.135.83:11440/api/tags
  → Set: export VITE_OLLAMA_URL='http://localhost:11434' (if local)

"Interrupted at question 45/100"
  → Resume: ./gaia-cli.sh resume --questions 100
  → Checkpoint auto-saved, continues from Q46

"JSONL validation failed"
  → Check: head -5 gaia-results.jsonl | jq .
  → Regenerate: ./gaia-cli.sh run --questions 50

"HF token rejected"
  → Get new: https://huggingface.co/settings/tokens
  → Export: export HF_TOKEN='hf_your_token'
  → Verify: curl -H "Authorization: Bearer $HF_TOKEN" https://huggingface.co/api/user
