STAGE-SPECIFIC FORMATTING (How to Apply)

Phase 1: Research
  Highlights: [EVIDENCE], [KEY]
  Callouts: TIP (methodology), WARNING (gaps)
  Charts: Distribution pie, timeline
  Badges: [Complete], [High Confidence]

Phase 2: Objections
  Highlights: [WARN], [IMPORTANT]
  Callouts: WARNING (common), CRITICAL (blockers)
  Charts: Stacked bar, heatmap
  Badges: [Risk High/Med/Low], [Opportunity]

Phase 3: Taste
  Highlights: [INSIGHT]
  Callouts: TIP (creative), SUCCESS (alignment)
  Charts: Color swatches, radar
  Badges: [Tone], [Style], [Audience]

Phase 4: Make
  Highlights: [KEY], [INSIGHT]
  Callouts: SUCCESS (wins), WARNING (trade-offs)
  Charts: Concept matrix, bar chart
  Badges: [Concept], [Positioning]

Phase 5: Test
  Highlights: [KEY], [EVIDENCE]
  Callouts: SUCCESS (winner), CRITICAL (failed)
  Charts: Ranking bar, confidence gauge
  Badges: [Winner], [High/Med/Low]

Phase 6: Memories
  Highlights: [INSIGHT]
  Callouts: SUCCESS (learned), WARNING (avoid)
  Charts: Timeline, trend line
  Badges: [Learning], [Win/Loss], [Repeat]
