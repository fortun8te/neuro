COMPONENT OVERVIEW
================================================================================

SEMANTICHIGHLIGHT:
  Purpose: Inline text highlighting for emphasis
  Types: key | warn | insight | evidence | note
  Example:
    <SemanticHighlight type="key">Critical insight</SemanticHighlight>

CALLOUTBOX:
  Purpose: Block callout containers
  Types: tip | warning | critical | success | quote
  Example:
    <CalloutBox type="warning">Important notice</CalloutBox>

BADGE:
  Purpose: Inline categorization tags
  Types: 24 semantic variants (research, market, priority, status, etc.)
  Example:
    <Badge type="research">Research</Badge>

DATATABLE:
  Purpose: Display and sort structured data
  Features: Click headers to sort, custom rendering, responsive
  Example:
    <DataTable columns={columns} rows={data} isDarkMode={isDarkMode} />

PROGRESSINDICATOR:
  Purpose: Show progress toward goals
  Types: ProgressBar (linear) | CircularProgress (ring)
  Example:
    <ProgressBar value={78} label="Research Coverage" />
